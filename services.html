Our Services

Comprehensive care for your beloved dogs

Forest Walks

Our forest walks provide your dogs with the opportunity to explore nature in a safe and supervised environment. We take dogs through beautiful forest trails where they can experience new sights, sounds, and smells that stimulate their minds and bodies.

  • Professional guides trained in dog behavior
  • Safe, leashed walks in natural environments
  • Small groups to ensure individual attention
  • Regular water breaks and rest periods

Pet Monitoring

Our 24/7 pet monitoring system ensures your dogs are always safe and cared for. We use advanced monitoring technology combined with human supervision to keep track of your pet's activities and well-being at all times.

  • Continuous surveillance throughout the facility
  • Trained staff on duty 24/7
  • Regular health and behavior checks
  • Immediate response to any concerns

Daily Updates & Photos

Stay connected with your pet through our daily update service. We send regular photos and updates about your dog's activities, meals, and overall well-being, giving you peace of mind while you're away.

  • Daily photo updates via email or app
  • Detailed activity reports
  • Feeding and medication logs
  • Behavioral observations and notes

Rewards System

Our positive reinforcement rewards system encourages good behavior and helps dogs feel loved and appreciated. We work with owners to establish appropriate rewards based on their preferences and their dog's needs.

  • Customized reward plans per owner agreement
  • Healthy treats and praise for good behavior
  • Training reinforcement through positive methods
  • Progress tracking and achievement celebrations

Delivery Service

Our convenient door-to-door delivery service ensures your pet travels safely and comfortably between your home and our facility. We provide professional transportation with proper safety measures.

  • Door-to-door pickup and drop-off
  • Climate-controlled vehicles
  • Experienced pet transport staff
  • Flexible scheduling options

24/7 Veterinary Services

We have partnered with local veterinary clinics to provide 24-hour emergency services. Your pet's health and safety are our top priorities, and we ensure immediate medical attention when needed.

  • 24/7 emergency veterinary care available
  • Regular health check-ups included
  • Partnership with trusted local veterinarians
  • Immediate response to medical emergencies
